About this game

Hello! This game has no title yet please suggest one I am so terribly bad with names.

I was inspired by:

  • Kittens Game, a silly but complex civilization simulator with a great theme.
  • A Dark Room, an incredible story told extremely simply.
  • Universal Paperclips, an excellent exploration of its topic and a poignant statement about it and more.
  • Candy box, possibly the first of its kind and incredibly influential.

plus plenty of other, less related things that I don't have room to mention. I highly recommend playing all of these games if you haven't already.
